Tektronix Due Diligence Pilot — Consent and Messaging Use Case

Last updated: 2026-04-18

This page documents the messaging use case, consent model, and compliance posture for a private technical due diligence pilot. It is intended to support toll-free SMS verification and carrier review for a low-volume, authorized-user workflow.

Business and pilot summary

Program

Tektronix Due Diligence Pilot

Volume

Low volume pilot, approximately 100 messages per month or fewer.

Audience

Only pre-authorized internal pilot users. No public enrollment.

Messaging use case

This toll-free number is used for a private pilot workflow for technical due diligence request intake. An authorized internal pilot user sends a short request message, such as report nvidia santa clara ae. The system accepts the request only if the sender phone number matches an active allowlisted contact. Unknown or inactive phone numbers are rejected automatically before any request is processed.

Not a marketing program.

This workflow is not used for promotions, lead generation, cold outreach, bulk campaigns, or purchased-contact messaging.

Consent / opt-in model

Consent is collected through a manual, administrator-managed internal pilot enrollment process.

  • Users are added individually by an administrator before messaging is allowed.
  • Only known, approved pilot users may send messages to the number.
  • The sender phone number must match the approved contact record exactly.
  • There is no public keyword-based signup, no website signup form, and no mass opt-in list.
Proof of consent:

Participation is restricted to manually approved internal pilot users. Consent is tied to explicit administrator approval and allowlist enrollment before any messaging workflow can begin.

report nvidia santa clara ae

Outbound confirmation example

Tektronix pilot: your due diligence request for NVIDIA Santa Clara has been received and queued for processing. The completed report will be sent to your authorized Tektronix email. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

Outbound completion example

Tektronix pilot: your due diligence report is ready and has been sent to your Tektronix email. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help.

Compliance and controls

  • Only authorized internal pilot users may participate.
  • Unknown or inactive numbers are denied automatically.
  • Messages are one-to-one and request-driven.
  • No public marketing or list growth activity is performed.
  • Reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help.
